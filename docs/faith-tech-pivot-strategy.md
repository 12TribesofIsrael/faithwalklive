## Phase 1 — Faith Walk Live (next 30 days)

**Goal:** Expand the existing tracker into a standalone companion platform that honors the walk, ships under our own banner, and doubles as portfolio proof.

### Positioning
- **Name:** Faith Walk Live (tentative — also considering "Walk With Zay" or "The 3000")
- **Tagline:** *"A believer's companion to the Philly→Cali Faith Walk"*
- **Explicit positioning:** supporter-built, not official HMBL. No logo, no claim of affiliation. Just: "Built by a believer riding with the mission."

### Features to add on top of current tracker
1. **Daily scripture card** — auto-rotates a scripture relevant to walking, perseverance, faith
2. **Live clip archive** — all daily clips viewable in one grid, filterable by type (Faith / Milestone / Support / Struggle)
3. **Prayer wall** — anyone can submit a prayer for Zay/the walk (moderated)
4. **Viewer map** — pin where supporters are watching from (opt-in)
5. **"Why this walk matters" page** — our testimony of why we're documenting this, no salesy language
6. **Subscribe form** — email list for daily walk updates (builds OUR audience)

### Tech decisions (keep it simple)
- Stay on GitHub Pages for now (free, already working)
- Add a lightweight backend only if the prayer wall / email list demands it (Modal, Vercel, or Supabase free tier)
- Own the domain: `faithwalklive.com` or similar (check availability)

### Social strategy
- Post the platform to your own IG/TikTok — don't need HMBL reshare
- Natural language: *"I built something to honor what Zay's doing. Check it out if you're following the walk."*
- Link in bio, drop the URL in Twitch chat during stream when the Spirit moves — don't spam

### What we DO NOT do
- ❌ Push it in HMBL Discord general chat (violates Cierra's earlier warning)
- ❌ Position it as "the official tracker" (it isn't)
- ❌ Trash HMBL's mods or the politics anywhere public
